Help & Support

General Rules for All Users


  • Only registered students with a verified university email can buy or sell.
  • No illegal, counterfeit, dangerous, or prohibited items.
  • We comply with the Equality Act 2010 and prioritise accessibility and inclusion.

Community Guidelines


  • Treat all members respectfully.
  • Communicate politely and clearly.
  • No pressuring, spamming, or lowballing.
  • Meet only at university-approved collection points.
  • Transactions are cash-only at point of exchange using our code verification system.

Buyer & Seller Guidelines


  • Buyers: Check item details and confirm prices. No off-platform deals.
  • Sellers: Provide honest listings and meet safely. No harassment or misleading practices.

Abuse & Misconduct


  • No illegal content, harassment, or code tampering.
  • No selling outside the platform post-contact.
  • Violations = suspension or account removal.

Data Protection & Privacy


We follow the Data Protection Act 2018 & UK GDPR. No personal data is shared without consent.


Complaints & Liability


Contact support for issues. The university mediates serious disputes but is not responsible for personal transactions unless rules are breached. Exchanges are at your own risk.

Privacy Policy


Brighton Student Marketplace Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 16, 2025


At Brighton Student Marketplace, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR.


What Data We Collect


  • Name
  • University email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Item listings, images & descriptions
  • Collection point preferences

How We Use Your Data


  • To manage your account and transactions
  • To connect buyers and sellers
  • To send important updates (order confirmations, safety advice)
  • To improve platform features and security
  • To comply with legal requirements

Your Rights


  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to how we use your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact Us


For privacy-related questions or requests: studentmarketplace@university.ac.uk

Marketplace Policies

  • Respectful buyer behaviour is mandatory — harassment or scams = suspension.
  • Once a sale is agreed, no interference or outbidding other buyers.
  • All transactions must stay within Brighton Student Marketplace.
  • No malicious or spam messaging — this results in account suspension.

Additional Commitments

  • Accessibility: We’re inclusive and accessible — use clear, inclusive communication.
  • Sustainability: We encourage reselling to reduce waste and promote sustainability.
  • Safety First: Meet in public, well-lit university locations and never share bank details.

Violating rules = possible suspension or removal from the platform.

FAQs

Browse listings, message the seller, agree on a price and collection point.
How do I list an item?
Tap ‘Sell an item,’ upload a photo, set a price and description.
How do I contact a seller?
Use the chat or messaging system .
